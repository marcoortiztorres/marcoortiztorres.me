Maia Miata
 October 2017 - PRESENT
Description:
 I am currently building my own "smart car" that I have named Maia Miata. Maia use data collected from a tuning ECU (megasquirt) determines the most efficient way to tune the car in order to preform a specific task. For example, in regular driving condidtion Maia will re-tune the car to get the best MPG, but in a track condition Maia will re-tune the car to get the most speed.
