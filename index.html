About. Marco A. Ortiz Torres
 I am Marco A. Ortiz Torres, above is an image of my engineering projects group with me standing on the far right. I am a sophomore at the University of Colorado studying Computer Science with a strong interest in artificial intelligence. I hope to one day make an AI system capable of natural flowing conversation so the people asking questions can become more interested in what they are learning.

Beyond my education, I have many interests including photography, cinematography, music production, and graphic design. Many of my projects are included on this site and can be found on the sites linked above. I unfortunately do not upload any of my music but you can always ask me to free style if you see me in person.

Thank you for visiting my page if you have some extra time read The Last Question by Isaac Asimov. It is a very interesting read and one of my favourite stories.
